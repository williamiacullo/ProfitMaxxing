Questions

Before you ask.

What's the catch with the free scan?
No catch. We analyze your publicly available data, benchmark it against your category, and send you the findings. If you find it valuable and want us to go deeper with your actual financial data, the full audit is $500 with a money-back guarantee. Most founders are surprised by what we find just from public data alone.
How is this different from Triple Whale or Lifetimely?
Those are data tools — they show you numbers. We're a decision tool. Every insight comes with market benchmarks, proof, and a specific action to take. We don't give you a dashboard to figure out. We tell you what to do.
What data access do you need?
Read-only API access to Shopify, your ad platforms (Meta/Google), and your most recent 3PL invoice. Takes 5 minutes to set up. We never write, modify, or store your raw data.
I already have a bookkeeper / accountant.
Good — you should. We don't replace your bookkeeper. They record what happened. We analyze what it means, benchmark it against the market, and tell you how to improve it. Think of us as the layer between your bookkeeper and a CFO.
Where does the benchmark data come from?
Multiple sources including Varos, SimilarWeb, marketplace data, and our own aggregated (anonymized) client base. Every benchmark includes the source and sample size so you can verify it yourself.
How long until I see the first report?
7 days from the day you grant data access. Most of that time is us doing the analysis — your total time investment is about 5 minutes for setup.
